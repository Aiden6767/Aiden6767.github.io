Scrambled Egg Recipe

Here is a recipe for scrambled eggs.

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

method

    Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds as if you are making an omelette. when the eggs are still moist remove from the heat, it will continue to cook on the plate while being served.
