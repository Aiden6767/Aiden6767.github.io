Grandma's Lemon Mirengue Pie

One 9-Inch Pie

30 Min - Prep Time

10 Min - Cook Time

40 Min - Total

8 Servings

INGRIDIENTS
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flower
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolkes, beaten
  • 1 9-inch pie crust baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

DIRECTIONS
  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Make Lemon Filling: In a medium sauce pan...
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar, flower, cornstarch, and salt.
